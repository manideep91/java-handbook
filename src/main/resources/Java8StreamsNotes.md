| **What it does**       | Transforms **each element** before collecting.                                                                                                                                                                | Transforms the **entire collected result** after collection.                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| **When to use**        | When you donâ€™t want the whole object, just a property or derived value.                                                                                                                                       | When the downstream gives you a type you want to post-process (e.g., `Optional`, `Long`, mutable list).                                                                                                                                                            |
